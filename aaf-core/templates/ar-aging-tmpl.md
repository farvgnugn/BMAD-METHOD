### Collection Method Effectiveness
```markdown
## Collection Strategy by Aging Bucket

### 1-30 Days Past Due (Friendly Reminder Stage)
- [ ] **Automated Email Reminders:** Day 5, 15, 25 past due
- [ ] **Phone Courtesy Call:** Day 10 past due
- [ ] **Statement Delivery:** Monthly statement with aging detail
- [ ] **Early Payment Incentive:** Offer small discount for immediate payment
- [ ] **Payment Plan Option:** Flexible payment arrangements if needed

### 31-60 Days Past Due (Active Collection Stage)
- [ ] **Collection Call:** Direct contact with accounts payable
- [ ] **Collection Letter:** Formal collection letter via mail/email
- [ ] **Management Contact:** Speak with customer's management
- [ ] **Hold on New Orders:** Credit hold until payment received
- [ ] **Payment Agreement:** Formal written payment arrangement

### 61-90 Days Past Due (Intensive Collection Stage)
- [ ] **Executive Contact:** CEO/CFO level communication
- [ ] **Site Visit:** In-person meeting if geographically feasible
- [ ] **Legal Notice:** Formal demand letter from legal counsel
- [ ] **Credit Report:** Report delinquency to credit bureaus
- [ ] **Collection Agency Preparation:** Prepare file for outside collection

### 91+ Days Past Due (Final Collection/Write-off Stage)
- [ ] **Collection Agency Referral:** Transfer to professional collection agency
- [ ] **Legal Action Evaluation:** Assess cost-benefit of legal proceedings
- [ ] **Asset Investigation:** Search for attachable assets
- [ ] **Settlement Negotiation:** Negotiate reduced settlement amount
- [ ] **Write-off Consideration:** Evaluate for bad debt write-off
```

## Customization Instructions

Replace the following placeholders when using this template:

**Company Information:**
- `[Company Name]` - Your company name
- `[Name]` - Preparer or staff member names
- `[Date]` - Relevant dates and periods

**Financial Information:**
- `$[Amount]` - Specific dollar amounts
- `[%]` - Percentage values
- `[X]` - Numbers, days, scores
- `[X days]` - Specific timeframes

**Customer Information:**
- `[Customer Name]` - Specific customer names
- `[Invoice#]` - Invoice numbers
- `[Score]` - Credit scores and ratings
- `[History]` - Payment history descriptions

**Process Information:**
- `[Action]` - Specific action plans
- `[Reason]` - Dispute reasons and explanations
- `[Industry]` - Industry classifications
- `[Region]` - Geographic regions

This template provides a comprehensive framework for accounts receivable management that optimizes collections, manages customer credit risk, and accelerates cash flow through strategic receivables management.
