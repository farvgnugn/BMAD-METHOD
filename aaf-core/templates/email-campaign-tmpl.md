## Email Writing Best Practices

### Subject Line Formulas
```markdown
‚úÖ HIGH-PERFORMING Subjects:
- "{Number} {timeframe} tips for {desired outcome}"
- "Quick question about {relevant topic}"
- "{Specific result} in {timeframe}? Here's how..."
- "The #{1/3/5} mistake {customer type} make"
- "{First Name}, this reminded me of you"

‚ùå AVOID These Subjects:
- All caps or excessive punctuation
- Generic "Newsletter" or company updates
- Misleading or clickbait headlines
- Overly promotional language
```

### Email Structure Guidelines
```markdown
‚úÖ EFFECTIVE Structure:
- Personal greeting with first name
- Hook that creates interest in first sentence
- One main idea per email
- Clear, specific call-to-action
- Personal sign-off from real person

‚úÖ SCANNING-Friendly Format:
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points and numbered lists
- Subheadings to break up content
- Bold text for emphasis (sparingly)
- White space for easy reading
```

### Personalization Strategies
```markdown
- Use first name in subject and greeting
- Reference their signup source or interest
- Segment based on behavior or preferences
- Include location-specific information
- Reference their industry or role
- Mention their company size or type
```

## A/B Testing Elements

### High-Impact Test Variables
```markdown
1. **Subject Lines**: Different angles or emotional appeals
2. **Send Times**: Day of week and time of day
3. **From Names**: Company vs. personal name
4. **Email Length**: Short vs. long-form content
5. **CTA Buttons**: Text, color, placement
6. **Images**: With vs. without visuals
7. **Personalization**: Level of personal details
8. **Content Format**: Story vs. tips vs. case study
```

## Performance Metrics

### Email Campaign KPIs
```markdown
**Delivery Metrics:**
- Delivery rate (emails delivered)
- Bounce rate (hard vs. soft bounces)
- Spam complaint rate

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Open rate (industry benchmarks vary)
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Click-to-open rate (CTOR)
- Time spent reading

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Conversion rate to landing page
- Revenue per email sent
- Unsubscribe rate
- List growth rate
```
